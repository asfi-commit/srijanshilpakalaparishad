FOUNDER'S MESSAGE

Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad is devoted to upheld the true elegance of cultural diversity in India. The organization is bound to encourage real talents from every corner of the Society . The agenda is to focus on the cultural heritage of India through providing every kind of artist a platform where they can connect with the Government and ask for their better future. As a Founder & President , it is my request to common people to come forward and tell us about those artists who have such a talent that needs to be showcased among the world. Thank you for being with us and I hope you will be our constant support throughout this journey.

Smt. Chhanda Chowdhury.
Founder, Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad.
