FOUNDER'S MESSAGE

Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad is devoted to uphold the true elegance of cultural diversity in India. The organization looks forward to encourage real talents from every corner of the society. The agenda is to focus on the cultural heritage of India by providing every kind of artist a platform where they can connect with the Government and pave their way towards a better future. As the Founder & President, it is my request to everyone to come forward and tell us about those artists who have such talents that need to be showcased to the world. Thank you for being with us and I hope you will be our constant support throughout this journey.

Smt. Chhanda Chowdhury.
Founder, Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad.
