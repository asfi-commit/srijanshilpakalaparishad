ABOUT US

Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad is a trusted organization since 2021 . It is a complete Examination board of Fine art & Culture. Painting, Dance, Music, Recitation, Yoga, Strengthening the infrastructure of various subjects. To spread the Indian cultural ideology all over the world and to establish new emerging artists. Our thinking is to develop new ideas of the youth society, to move forward with their respect in the society. Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad is a trusted platform for us to be established to pave the way for a brighter and long-term future for all the cultural organizations and Institutions which are associated with our Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad family. Although the extension of Indian art & craft has surpassed the borders across the world, still there is scarcity of a organization for the development of students in a right manner. Which is the reason why the students from common and lower middle class family cannot aspire the desired dreams. And the desire to make their dreams true is what made our Foundation growing.

Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad is an ISO 9001 : 2015 Certified Organisation. It was registered under West Bengal Societies Registration Act, Indian Trust Act, MSME , NITI Ayog & Copy Right Act under Government of India.

We are proud and joyful to say that our Organisation had well distributed itself within this time and also garnered appreciations from the students and their families by being culturally associated.

OUR MISSION

Students who are taking cultural education for the betterment of the cultural world so that they can become famous artists of the future. They are receiving training on various cultural issues We are always on the lookout for those things that can be learned by reading them thoroughly.

OUR VISION

  1. To provide free cultural training to the poor and destitute students
  2. The same applies to students with disabilities
  3. To start reading the theories of the students so that they can know the depth of every branch, every branch, starting from the root of cultural subjects.
