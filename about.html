ABOUT US

Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad is a trusted organization that began working since 2021. It is a complete Examination board of Fine art & Culture, including the niches of Painting, Dance, Music, Vocals, Recitation, and Yoga, that looks to strengthen the infrastructure of various subjects. Our aim is to spread the Indian cultural ideology all over the world and to establish new emerging artists. Our thinking is to develop new ideas of the youth society, so that they can successfully move forward with respect. We are a trusted platform to pave the way for a brighter and long-term future for all the cultural organizations and institutions which are associated with our Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad family. Although the extension of Indian art & craft has surpassed the borders across the world, there still exists a scarcity of an organization for the development of students in the right manner. And, that is why the students from lower and lower-middle class families cannot aspire to fulfill their desired dreams. And the desire to make their dreams true is what made our foundation grow.

Srijan Shilpa Kala Parishad is an ISO 9001 : 2015 Certified Organisation. It was registered under West Bengal Societies Registration Act, Indian Trust Act, MSME , NITI Ayog & Copy Right Act under Government of India.

We are proud and elated to say that our Organisation has well distinguished itself within this short span of time and also garnered huge appreciation from our culturally associated students and their families.

OUR MISSION

We encourage students to take cultural education for the betterment of the cultural world and for them to become famous artists in the future. They receive training on various cultural issues by being on the lookout for those things that can be learned by reading thoroughly.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to provide free cultural training to the poor, destitute and specially-abled students under our care.
And, we encourage students so that they can know the depth of every branch of art, starting right from the origin of every cultural subject.
